Your OpenSky, ADS-B Exchange, and AviationStack credentials are stored locally in your browser’s sessionStorage. We do not transmit, log, or store your credentials on our server beyond forwarding them (only once per query) to the respective Third-Party API. All flight-detection logs (timestamp, callsign, azimuth, etc.) are stored in your browser’s localStorage. These logs remain on your device and are never transmitted to the server. You may export or delete your local logs at any time via the “Download log” or “Clear log” functions.
